Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

PBNZ Barbers ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you submit a booking enquiry or contact form
  • Feedback provided through our private feedback form
  • Basic usage data through analytics (page views, device type)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your appointments
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To improve our services based on feedback
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing

3. Data Storage

Contact form submissions are processed via StaticForms. Your data is stored securely and not shared with unauthorised parties.

4. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access and correct your personal information. Contact us to make a request.

5. Cookies

This website may use basic cookies for functionality purposes. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at paul.singh7@hotmail.com or call 0210 247 4107.
